Timeline of US Constitution

An ideology comprised and founded by underdogs


1775-1776

War time!!! The chaos of monarchy and oppression caused the Thirteen Colonies to revolt against Britain. Declaration of Independence written and approved.


1781-1783

Revolutionary War ends, and the Thirteen Colonies won. The Continental Congress agrees on the Articles of Confederation. It is then replaced by the Congress of the Confederation, which serves as the cornerstone to the new US government. Britain recognizes the independence through the Treaty of Paris.


1787-1789

The US Constitution is written at the Constitutional Convention in 1781. The ratification proceeds by 9 states in 1788 and becomes the supreme law of the USA When George Washington got elected as the first president, the first Congress.


1790-1791

The First Supreme Court is assembled in NY until the US capital moves to Philadelphia The first 10 amendments in the Bill of Rights are added


1800-1802

The US capital moves to Washington. Under Chief Marshall's judgement, Marbury v. Madison is the first time that the court found that a law was unconstitutional


1861-1863

Civil War begun and ended on 1865. Amendment 13: abolishes slavery. Abraham Lincoln is assassinated


1868-1870

Amendment 14: Rights of citizenship to all people born in USA or naturalized 1870 Amendment 15: Voting rights to all citizens, regardless of color or race, but women are not mentioned.
